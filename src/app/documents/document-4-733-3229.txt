LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT

I, [John Doe], of [1234 Main Street, Springfield, State], being of sound mind and memory and not acting under duress or undue influence, and fully understanding the nature and extent of all my property and of this disposition thereof, do hereby make, publish, and declare this document to be my Last Will and Testament, hereby revoking any and all other wills and codicils previously made by me.

ARTICLE I - DECLARATION
I am currently married to [Jane Doe] and have three (3) children: [Alice Doe], [Bob Doe], and [Charlie Doe]. In this Will, any reference to "my children" includes [Alice Doe], [Bob Doe], and [Charlie Doe] collectively.

ARTICLE II - EXECUTOR
I appoint [Jane Doe] as the Executor of this Will. If [Jane Doe] is unable or unwilling to serve, then I appoint [Bob Doe] as the successor Executor. My Executor shall have all the powers permissible under the laws of [State], including but not limited to the power to sell, lease, or mortgage real estate at public or private sale.

ARTICLE III - BEQUESTS

Personal Property
I bequeath all of my household furniture, clothing, jewelry, personal automobiles, and other tangible personal property to my spouse, [Jane Doe]. If she does not survive me, these items shall be distributed equally among my children.
Real Estate
I devise my property located at [1234 Main Street, Springfield, State], including all improvements thereon and appurtenances thereto, to my spouse, [Jane Doe]. Should [Jane Doe] predecease me, this property shall be divided equally among my children. Each child shall have the right to live in the property as long as they choose or until they decide to sell their share.
Investments and Financial Accounts
I bequeath all my shares, securities, and bank accounts to my children, to be divided equally among them. This includes any investments in stocks, bonds, mutual funds, and other securities.
ARTICLE IV - GUARDIANSHIP
If any of my children are under the age of eighteen (18) at the time of my passing, I appoint [Jane Doe] as guardian of the person and estate of the minor child(ren). If [Jane Doe] is unable to serve as guardian, I appoint [Alice Doe] as successor guardian.

ARTICLE V - TAXES AND EXPENSES
All estate, inheritance, and succession taxes (including any interest and penalties thereon) payable by reason of my death shall be paid out of and charged generally against my estate. The Executor shall have the authority to employ other persons as may be necessary in the administration of my estate and to compensate them from the estate assets.

ARTICLE VI - MISCELLANEOUS

I hereby direct that my remains be cremated and my ashes be scattered at [specific location, e.g., Pacific Ocean].
If any beneficiary under this Will contests in any court any of the provisions herein, then such beneficiary shall forfeit his or her right to any portion of my estate.
ARTICLE VII - ATTESTATION
This Will was signed on the [date], at [location], by [John Doe] as and for his Last Will and Testament, in the presence of us, who at his request, in his presence, and in the presence of each other, have subscribed our names as witnesses.
