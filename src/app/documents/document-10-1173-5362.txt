RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Lender agrees to loan a certain amount to the Borrower, and the Borrower agrees to repay this amount under the terms set forth in this Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parties intend to set forth the terms and conditions upon which such loan will be made and repaid;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises herein contained, the Parties hereto agree as follows:

1. LOAN AMOUNT AND DISBURSEMENT

1.1 Loan Amount. The Lender agrees to loan the Borrower the principal sum of [Loan Amount in words] dollars ($[Loan Amount in numbers]) (the "Loan").

1.2 Disbursement. The Loan will be disbursed to the Borrower's bank account within [number] business days following the execution of this Agreement and completion of all necessary formalities.

2. INTEREST RATE

2.1 Interest Rate. The Loan shall bear interest at an annual rate of [Interest Rate]% compounded [monthly/annually]. Interest will be calculated on a 365-day year basis, beginning on the date the Loan is disbursed.

3. REPAYMENT TERMS

3.1 Repayment Schedule. The Borrower agrees to repay the Loan in [number] [monthly/quarterly/annual] installments of [Installment Amount] dollars ($[Installment Amount]), beginning on [Start Date] and thereafter on the [day] of each [month/quarter/year].

3.2 Prepayment. The Borrower may prepay the Loan in whole or in part at any time without penalty. Any prepayment will first be applied to accrued interest and then to the principal amount.

4. USE OF LOAN FUNDS

4.1 Use of Funds. The Borrower agrees to use the Loan exclusively for [describe purpose, e.g., business expansion, purchase of equipment, etc.].

5. LATE PAYMENTS

5.1 Late Payment Penalty. If any installment payment is not paid within [number] days after its due date, the Borrower shall pay a late fee of [Late Fee Amount] dollars ($[Late Fee Amount]) or [Percentage]% of the overdue amount, whichever is greater.

6. DEFAULT

6.1 Events of Default. The occurrence of any of the following shall constitute an event of default ("Event of Default") under this Agreement:

Failure by the Borrower to make any payment when due under this Agreement;
Breach of any other covenant or agreement in this Agreement by the Borrower;
Bankruptcy or insolvency of the Borrower;
Any representation or warranty made by the Borrower in this Agreement proves to have been incorrect or misleading in any material respect when made.
6.2 Remedies on Default. Upon the occurrence of an Event of Default, the Lender may declare the entire outstanding balance of the Loan (including accrued interest and any other charges) immediately due and payable. The Lender may exercise this right without notice to the Borrower.

7. SECURITY

7.1 Collateral. As security for repayment of the Loan, the Borrower agrees to pledge to the Lender [describe collateral, e.g., real estate, personal property, etc.] (the "Collateral").

7.2 Security Agreement. The terms of the pledge shall be set forth in a separate Security Agreement that the Parties will execute concurrently with this Loan Agreement.

8. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of [State], without regard to its conflict of law provisions.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

In the event of a dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement, the Parties will attempt to resolve the dispute through friendly consultation. If the dispute is not resolved within a reasonable period then any or all outstanding issues may be submitted to mediation in accordance with any statutory rules of mediation. If mediation is not successful in resolving the entire dispute, any outstanding issues will be submitted to final and binding arbitration under the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The arbitrator's award will be final, and judgment may be entered upon it by any court having jurisdiction within the State of [State].

10. MISCELLANEOUS

10.1 Amendments. This Agreement may only be amended or modified by a written document executed by both Parties.

10.2 Notices. All notices, requests, demands, and other communications under this Agreement shall be in writing and properly addressed to the Parties at the addresses set forth at the beginning of this Agreement.

10.3 Severability. If any part of this Agreement is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining parts will continue to be valid and enforceable.

10.4 Entire Agreement. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes any prior understandings or agreements, oral or written, relating to the subject matter of this Agreement.

10.5 Counterparts. This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, but all of which together will constitute one and the same instrument.
